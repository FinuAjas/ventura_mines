About Us

VENTURA MINING LIMITED was established in 2022 from a strong partnership between SOUTH POLE Inc., a well-known Indian equipment and general trade Company in Liberia and Saudi Arabia and GHR GHANA LIMITED, a highly successful MINING SERVICES to various mining companies in Ghana and the Republic of Guinea.
Since 2012 we supply mining contracting, earthworks, logistics and construction services to some major mining companies in Ghana and Guinea and we are a key player in the Forestry Industry in Liberia, supplying timber products to the world since 2016.
