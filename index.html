About Us

Our company has been working with the mining and logistics industry for more than a decade/since 2012 with branches across West Africa and Saudi Arabia. Over the past decade we have not only improved entire plant processes, but also developed a comprehensive portfolio of mining services ranging from long-term service agreements to individual service products like spare parts, engineering and logistics.

We are a key player in the Forestry Industry by supplying timber products to the world. Together with our customers and partners, we introduce state-of-the-art predictive maintenance tools as well as solutions for upgrades and retrofits.
